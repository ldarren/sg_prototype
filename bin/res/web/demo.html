What Am I Looking At?

This is a WebView (the analog would be a standalone 'tab' in Firefox or Chrome) that is actively rendering to a texture and being projected onto a full-screen overlay.

Naturally, we're not limited to just overlays-- hit F1 to see!

Controls

  • Esc - Quit
  • F1 - Toggle 3D Mode
  • F2 - Toggle Transparency
  • F3 - Return to this page
  • F4 - Increase Page Zoom by 20%
  • F5 - Decrease Page Zoom by 20%
  • F6 - Print FPS to Console
  • F7 - Toggle Shadows (default disabled)

Extra Controls for 3D Mode

  • Hold right-mouse-button and drag to rotate camera around
  • Use W A S D keys to move around the terrain (while the WebView isn't focused)
